Hileon Budget Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026

Hileon Budget Tracker is developed by Hileon, based in Zimbabwe. This policy explains exactly how we handle your information. Short version: your financial data never leaves your device.

Core Principles

Overview

Your financial data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal financial information.

Hileon Budget Tracker ("the App") is an Android application for personal budgeting, business finance tracking, invoice management, and project cost tracking. This Privacy Policy explains what data is involved when you use the App and how it is handled.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All of the following is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and never transmitted to any external server:

  • Personal expenses, income, categories, notes and dates
  • Monthly budgets and spending history
  • Business income, expenses, invoices and inventory
  • Business profile information (name, phone, email, address)
  • Project budgets, phases, contributions and goals
  • Client and supplier details saved for invoice auto-fill
  • App preferences — language, currency, theme
  • Profile name (if entered by the user)
Data & Third Parties

Data That Leaves the Device

The only information that ever leaves your device is when you voluntarily redeem a promotional/affiliate code. In that case, the code string is sent over HTTPS to a secure endpoint and logged for affiliate tracking purposes.

What is sent Why Stored where Personal data?
Promo code string only Affiliate tracking Private Google Sheet (Hileon only) No

No financial data, no device identifiers, and no personal information of any kind is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Hileon uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data involved
Google Play Billing Subscription & purchase processing Handled entirely by Google. We never see payment card details.
Capacitor (Ionic) Local notifications, file system Local device only. No data transmitted.

Hileon contains no advertising SDKs. There are zero advertisements in the app, ever.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Subscriptions & Payments

Hileon offers optional subscription plans. All purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Prices are shown in your local currency at checkout.

  • We do not collect, store, or process payment card information
  • Subscriptions are managed through your Google Play account
  • Cancel at any time via Google Play → Subscriptions
  • Purchases restore automatically when you reinstall the app
Your Rights & Controls

Data Export & Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

Export as CSV
Delete individual records

Request deletion by email

No Account Required

Hileon does not require you to create an account, verify an email address, or provide any personal identity information to use the app. All features are available completely anonymously.

Additional Policies

Children's Privacy

Hileon is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us immediately at hileonbt@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

International Users

Hileon is designed for users across Africa and worldwide — with particular focus on Zimbabwe, Mozambique, South Africa, Kenya, and Ethiopia. The App supports 6 languages and 150+ currencies. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location.
