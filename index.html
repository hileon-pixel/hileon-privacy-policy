Privacy Policy — Effective March 27, 2026

📋 Overview

Hileon Budget Tracker ("the App") is developed and operated by Hileon, based in Mutare, Zimbabwe. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

Short version: All your financial data is stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal financial information to anyone.

📱 Data We Store Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage:

  • Personal expenses, categories, notes and dates
  • Business income, expenses and invoices
  • Budget settings and monthly budget amounts
  • Business profile information (name, phone, email, address)
  • Client details saved for invoice auto-fill
  • App preferences (language, currency, theme)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server operated by us.

☁️ Data Collected by Third Parties

The App integrates the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases (Remove Ads, Business Premium). Purchase data is handled by Google Play. Google Payments Privacy

💳 In-App Purchases

The App offers two optional one-time in-app purchases:

  • Remove Ads Forever — $4.99 USD
  • Business Premium — $9.99 USD

All payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not store or access your payment card details. Purchases are tied to your Google account and restore automatically when you reinstall the app or switch devices.

🔒 Data Security

Since all financial data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock (PIN, fingerprint or pattern)
  • Keeping your Android OS up to date
  • Using the CSV export feature to back up your data regularly

👶 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

🌍 International Users

Hileon Budget Tracker is designed for users across Africa and worldwide, including Zimbabwe, Mozambique, South Africa, Kenya, and Ethiopia. The App supports 6 languages and 150+ currencies. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location.

📤 Data Export & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export — Use the CSV Export feature in Settings to download all your data at any time
  • Delete — Clear all data by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Individual records — Delete any transaction, invoice or record individually within the App

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
